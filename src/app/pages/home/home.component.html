Help Hawaii

Global Surf Community Weekend Fundraiser

Friday 8th to Sunday 10th September 2023
For
Maui Island Community Disaster Relief Efforts

Help Hawaii

Local communities all over the world are coming together to send a wave of support to our Maui Island friends, family (Ohana) & community that has been absolutely devastated by the wildfires that tore through the island of Maui on 08 August 2023.

We welcome you to join us over 3 days, from Friday 8th to Sunday 10th September 2023. During this event packed weekend, Surf & Wellness communities all over the world are coming together to support those Hawaiian island families & communities directly impacted by the Maui fires

All funds raised will go directly to those impacted to help them rebuild their homes, their businesses, their jobs, their livelihoods, and their communities in partnership with local Maui organisations and relief efforts.

With your help & support, what started as a few community led fundraising events among surf communities in Ireland and Portugal, will spread far & wide through surf communities, coastal villages & towns, local businesses, individuals, families and friends all over the world.

Let us come together in solidarity & aloha with our Hawaiian brothers & sisters, and all those affected by these devastating fires on Maui. Let us share the spirit of Aloha with each other & our Maui Ohana & Community to help them heal and recover from this most difficult of times.

The Hawaiians brought the spirit of Aloha & surfing to the world. Now let us all come together to send a wave of love, support & aloha from local communities everywhere to the Hawaiian islands that are so dear to so many of us.

Mahalo Nui
