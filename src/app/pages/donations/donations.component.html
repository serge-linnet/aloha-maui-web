Donate

Event organisers & event participants can use the links below to make their donations directly to the non-profits and community relief efforts that are providing disaster relief, support and funding to those communities on Maui that are directly impacted by the wild fires.

A ʻohe hana nui ke alu ʻia.
No task is too big when done together by all.

ʻŌlelo Noʻeau 14

The funds donated to these local Maui organisations are being used to support the Maui community to provide immediate rapid response & recovery. Going forward, these non-profits will play a crucial role in supporting our Maui Ohana in rebuilding their homes, their businesses, their jobs, their livelihoods & their communities.
